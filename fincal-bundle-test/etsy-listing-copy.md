# Cash Runway Planner for Excel

Plan your cash runway before you run into a shortfall.

This manual cash runway planning template is built for freelancers and solo business owners who need a fast way to project recurring income, recurring bills, and one-off cash events without setting up a full finance system.

## What it helps you do
- Track recurring income and expenses
- Project your lowest upcoming balance
- See when cash gets tight
- Compare base, conservative, and optimistic scenarios
- Model one-off costs before they happen

## What's included
- Cash runway planner workbook for Excel
- Dashboard with lowest balance and ending balance KPIs
- Scenario planning sheets
- Quick start guide
- Compatibility and upgrade notes

## Best for
- Freelancers
- Solo business owners
- Small agencies
- Founders doing lightweight runway planning

## Not included
- Automatic transaction imports
- Bank sync
- Google Calendar sync
- Custom setup services

## Upgrade path
If you want the automated version of this workflow, FinCal imports transaction files, detects recurring cash events, and generates a forward-looking balance forecast.
