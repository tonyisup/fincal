Cash Runway Planner Quick Start

Who this is for
- Freelancers and solo business owners who want a lightweight way to plan cash runway manually.

What is included
- Cash runway planner workbook
- Scenario planning sheets
- Sample solo-business assumptions
- FinCal upgrade note

Setup in 5 minutes
1. Open `cash-runway-planner.xlsx` in desktop Excel.
2. Update the `Inputs` sheet with:
   - Starting cash
   - Forecast start month
   - Number of months to project
   - Warning threshold
3. Add recurring income items on `Recurring Income`.
4. Add recurring expenses on `Recurring Expenses`.
5. Add large one-off items on `One-Off Adjustments`.
6. Review `Forecast Timeline` and `Dashboard`.
7. Compare scenarios using `Scenario Base`, `Scenario Conservative`, and `Scenario Optimistic`.

What this planner does
- Projects monthly cash runway
- Helps you spot your lowest projected balance
- Lets you test one-off shocks before they happen

What this planner does not do
- It does not import bank data
- It does not detect recurring transactions automatically
- It does not sync with Google Calendar
- It does not replace accounting software

When to switch to FinCal
- You are tired of updating the spreadsheet manually
- You want recurring bill and paycheck detection from transaction exports
- You want a faster forecast workflow with less spreadsheet maintenance
